## PII Controls
Personally identifiable information must be encrypted at rest and in transit. Access to PII requires business justification, role approval, and quarterly review. PII exports are blocked unless approved by Compliance.

## GDPR Evidence
GDPR requests must be tracked with request ID, data subject, response owner, completion date, and evidence of deletion or export. Audit evidence is retained for seven years.

## Audit Requirements
Every sensitive data access event must include user ID, role, purpose, timestamp, source system, and approval reference. Exceptions must be reviewed within five business days.

## Ambiguous Terminology
Some older audit records use the phrase entitlement review instead of access review. Both refer to the quarterly control requiring reviewer approval, removal evidence, and exception tracking.
