## Authentication and MFA
All workforce users must enroll in phishing-resistant MFA for privileged systems. SMS-based MFA is permitted only for low-risk applications during transition periods. Shared accounts are prohibited except for approved break-glass accounts that are vaulted, monitored, and reviewed monthly.

## Data Classification
Public data may be shared externally after communications approval. Internal data is limited to employees and approved contractors. Confidential data includes financial forecasts, customer contracts, non-public roadmap information, and employee relations notes. Restricted data includes regulated personal data, authentication secrets, production credentials, and audit evidence.

## Endpoint Security
Company laptops must run managed endpoint protection, disk encryption, and patch management. Devices missing critical patches for more than fourteen days are quarantined from production administration networks. Exceptions require Security approval and a documented remediation date.

## Incident Evidence Handling
During security incidents, responders should preserve logs, alert IDs, affected user IDs, source IP addresses, and timeline notes. Evidence may be shared with Compliance and Legal but should not be posted in broad chat channels.
