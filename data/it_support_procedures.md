## Password Reset and Account Recovery
Employees should use the self-service identity portal for routine password resets. If the portal is unavailable, Service Desk may verify identity using the employee ID, manager name, and a recent ticket reference. Service Desk must not ask users to disclose passwords, MFA codes, or recovery seed phrases. Temporary access codes expire after 15 minutes.

## Laptop Replacement Procedure
Lost, stolen, or damaged laptops must be reported within one business day. The support technician records the device serial number, user department, last known network location, and whether confidential data may have been cached locally. Replacement devices are prioritized for customer support, payroll processing, and security operations roles.

## Production Access Requests
Production access requires manager approval, system-owner approval, and completion of security awareness training. Access should be granted through the access management platform rather than direct group edits. Emergency access is allowed for incident response but must be reviewed within two business days.

## Known Procedure Gap
The legacy VPN troubleshooting guide still references the retired "VPN Classic" client. Current employees should use SecureConnect. A migration note exists, but some older tickets still say "classic VPN", which can cause retrieval ambiguity.
