## Forecast Summary
The FY2026 operating forecast assumes enterprise renewal growth of 9 to 11 percent and cloud infrastructure cost growth of 6 percent after optimization. The finance team is tracking a risk that support hiring may exceed plan if ticket backlog remains above target for two consecutive months.

## Cloud Cost Controls
Reserved instance coverage improved after the April right-sizing review, but analytics workloads still show high weekend utilization. Finance approved a follow-up review with IT to identify workloads that can move to lower-cost batch windows. The current monthly savings estimate is 42,000 USD, but that figure excludes data transfer charges.

## Payroll And Compensation Notes
Payroll modernization remains funded. Individual compensation records are not included in this operating report. Queries about specific salaries should be denied unless the requester has explicit HR, Finance leadership, or Admin access.

## Board Reporting Note
External reporting language must not cite unapproved forecast ranges. Internal finance users may reference the forecast summary, but public summaries should use approved investor-relations language only.
