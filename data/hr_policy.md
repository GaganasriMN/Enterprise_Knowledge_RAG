## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Remote work must be recorded in the workforce portal before 10 AM local time. Customer-facing teams need coverage approval before taking remote days.

## Leave Policy
Full-time employees receive twenty paid leave days per calendar year. Sick leave is available for medical needs and should be reported through the HR portal. Leave requests longer than five working days require manager and HR approval.

## Conduct and Data Handling
Employees must not share confidential customer, payroll, finance, or audit information outside approved systems. Policy violations must be reported to HR or Compliance within two business days.
