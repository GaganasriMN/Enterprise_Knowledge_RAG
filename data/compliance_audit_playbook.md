## Evidence Collection
Audit evidence must identify the control owner, system scope, sample period, reviewer, approval reference, and retention location. Screenshots are acceptable only when exports are unavailable. Evidence should be stored in the compliance repository, not in personal drives or unmanaged chat threads.

## Access Review Control
Quarterly access reviews must include privileged users, production support groups, finance approvers, and data export administrators. Reviewers must mark each entitlement as approved, removed, or exception pending. Exceptions older than five business days require Compliance escalation.

## Privacy Request Handling
GDPR and privacy requests require request ID, data subject region, request type, response deadline, fulfillment owner, and evidence of deletion, export, or lawful refusal. Customer support notes are not sufficient proof unless linked to the privacy case.

## Known Audit Finding
The previous audit found inconsistent terminology between "access review" and "entitlement review". Compliance accepted both terms if the evidence includes approval status, reviewer identity, and completion date.
