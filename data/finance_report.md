## Q4 Revenue Summary
Q4 revenue was 18.4 million USD, which was 12 percent higher than Q3. Enterprise renewals contributed 6.1 million USD. New customer bookings contributed 4.3 million USD. The strongest region was North America.

## Cost and Budget Notes
Cloud infrastructure cost increased by 8 percent due to higher analytics workload volume. The finance team approved a 450,000 USD optimization program for the next quarter.

## Restricted Payroll Note
Payroll and individual compensation records are restricted to Finance leadership and Admin users. They must not be disclosed to general employees.

## Data Quality Note
The Q4 revenue summary is a management reporting snapshot. It should not be treated as audited financial statements. The final quarter-close packet may adjust renewal allocation and cloud cost accruals.
